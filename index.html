ABOUT US

The DNA project aims to establish a decentralized self-sovereign global identity system on a blockchain. The mission is to have higher officials, such as state or local government bodies, issue a global digital identification. Each country will have its private ledger with its access.

The identity is issued as an NFT (Non-Fungible Token) holding the identity information of the owner in the form of metadata. Organizations could use the NFT as legal documentation, financial report, and vendor relationship.
